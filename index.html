Our story

Who We Were When We Met

Lori

Feels like an eternity ago - when I met Ty I was pretty much going with the flow - my top priority was living life to it’s full potential. I was having fun with my friends, living my best life in Miami and traveling every chance I got. I was still in real estate full time and had no plans of getting out of it.

Tyson

You may ask who Tyson Jermain Barnes Blackwood was before he met Lori SimOne Harris. Well that's easy, I was a bachelor who spent most of my time hanging out with my boys, working, and heading to happy hours when I could. Also, I was and still am big into traveling the world. I enjoy different cultures, tasting different foods, and new experiences.

How We Met

We met on Bumble! - The dating app where the woman has to write the man first. Lori said “Hi there!”, and he ended up inviting her to a Happy Hour. When the time came, she mixed up their plans to meet up at Tarpon Bend with Public House (which is directly across the street). When he called to see where she was she demanded he come to Public House instead. He walked over to where she was, showed her the proof that they were not scheduled to meet there, and they’ve been together ever since :)

How We Grew Together

When we first started dating we spent a lot of time at the beach and going out to eat getting to know each other. We were embraced by each others families from the very beginning which was super sweet and a big plus. We’ve both grown in our careers, adopted our dog Lilo, traveled to 5 countries, and purchased our first home together.

Where We Want To Go Next 

 

First came love, then comes marriage, then comes the baby in the baby carriage! But for now, we look forward to buying our next home together. Our goal is and has always been to acquire investment properties and to travel the globe with our family.
